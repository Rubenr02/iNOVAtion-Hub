1. What is the purpose of this website?

This website is designed to be a platform for managing innovation, where people from all backgrounds can share their ideas and problems, and build those ideas to form a more complex project. Any idea can have the potential to receive funding, it's up to the idea initiator to progress the idea's level and attract potential investors. If interested, investors can contact the idea initiatiors to facilitate communication.

2. Who can use iNOVAtion?

Anyone with ideas and problems to share (and an email account) can join iNOVAtion. iNOVAtion offers an open environment, in which users can exchange ideas and have friendly discussions that contribute to improving the overall well-being of the community.

3. How do I create an account?

To create an account, simply select the "Sign In" option in the top of the landing page, and then click on "Register" to start creating your account. In case you were gifted a code for becoming a Reviewer, then you should use it here to gain access to this role.

4. How can I level up my idea?

Levels exist in order to display the current progress status of a project, and also to encourage users to develop their ideas more. Every idea starts at Level 1, and each level up will require you to include more detailed information about your idea. For your idea to be considered for a level-up, simply fill in a Request Form, and a reviewer will evaluate your form and check if your idea is apt to progress to the next level.

5. What kind of content is allowed on this website?

Any content that is relevant to the context of NOVA University and its community is allowed on this website. Keep in mind, it is necessary to always act in a respectful and appropriate manner. No form of violence, discrimination, or prejudice is allowed. Moderators are responsible for maintaining a warm and friendly environment, and anything and everyone that doesn't respect this will be removed from the platform.

6. How can I share my reviewer status?

To gift someone a Reviewer role, simply go to you account page and generate a code. Share that code with the person you feel that are appropriate to be a reviewer. They will have to insert the code when signing up or in their account page to become a reviewer.

7. How do I report inappropriate content?

To report inappropriate content, click on the "Report" button located next to the content and follow the instructions.

8. How do I become a reviewer?

To become a reviewer, someone that is already a reviewer has to invite you. This means that they will have to give you a code, that will give you reviewer status. Insert that code on your account page to gain access to the reviewer role.
